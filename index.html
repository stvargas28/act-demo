Adaptive Learning + Ambient Design

Two principles tie every ACT capability together. First, it learns continuously from what you actually do - not what you say you want. Second, it never asks you to come to it. It lives inside Teams, Outlook, Excel, and your calendar, surfacing only when it has something worth saying.

01
Watches what you do - not just what you click
02
Quiet daily review - compares suggestions vs reality
03
Personal profile - silently updated, never configured
04
Ambient presence - no new app to open, ever
05
Approval-first - trust built before autonomy granted
Design Principle
If the user has to "go use ACT" - you failed.

If ACT comes to the user with decisions - you win.

ACT is not an assistant you open.
It is a real-time coordination and execution layer powered by live system data and behavioural learning - one that works quietly in the background and surfaces only when it has something worth saying.
